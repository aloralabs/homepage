About Us

Alora Labs is a boutique development agency that brings innovation to business.

With deep technical and industry expertise, our team has developed custom products, ranging from wearables to apps to enterprise systems. We work with organizations of all scales and stripes to create products that solve problems and optimize user experience.

The Process

Plan

Get in touch for a no-charge consultation. We will discuss the challenges your business faces and what we can do to help.

Build

We start designing, coding, engineering, assembling and testing your project. We deliver early and often to ensure we're on the right track and keep communication channels open.

Scale

Once the project is usable and in the hands of some early users, it is typically a good time to step back and reflect on room for improvement. What can we do to elevate the project to the next level?
